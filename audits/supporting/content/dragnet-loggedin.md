## 300-word executive summary

The Almobadir network is **massive in reach but minimal in monetization infrastructure**. Across 7 Instagram handles, 4 TikTok handles, 3 YouTube channels, 3 Threads accounts, and one near-dead X account, the aggregated logged-in follower count exceeds **2.7M** (IG: 2.21M, TT: 1.11M, Threads: 169K, YouTube: 1.66K, X: 0.1K). The network's home-base content tilts heavily Moroccan (per the @almobadir-media YouTube archive featuring Simo Life and Anas Ouragh interviews, plus Morocco-specific tax/e-commerce topics) — even though the bio framing is pan-Arab. Brand naming drifts between platforms: the same sub-brand uses different Arabic taglines on IG vs TT.

**Highest-leverage moves visible from this dragnet:** (1) Launch a Substack or Beehiiv newsletter — every list-magnet post currently routes to an Instagram group invite, leaving zero owned email. (2) Stand up an Almobadir Media LinkedIn company page — a marketing agency without one is invisible to MENA B2B buyers. (3) Verify or quietly drop the unverifiable Master's-in-Marketing credential from Badr's bio. (4) Consolidate the brand-name drift across sub-handles. The bones of a 7-figure media business are here; the wiring is half-installed.
