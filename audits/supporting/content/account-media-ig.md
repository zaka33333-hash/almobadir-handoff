## 7 actionable insights for the website (esp. agency/services CTAs and the network grid)

### 1. The IG agency handle has no bio link — the website is the missing funnel destination

The single most important inbound channel from IG (the bio link) is empty. Every visitor who lands on @almobadir_media's profile has nowhere to go except a DM keyword or a frozen 2022 highlight. **The website should claim that real estate immediately.** Add `almobadirmedia.com/work` (or similar) to the bio and make the homepage a one-click path to:
- Case studies (which the IG feed completely lacks)
- Service pricing tiers
- Booking calendar (Calendly or a self-hosted equivalent)

### 2. The agency does not show its own work — the website must be the case-study library

Of the last 120 IG posts, ~2% are own client work. **All 5 all-time top posts are reaction reels about other brands' ads.** The website needs a `/case-studies` or `/portfolio` section as its primary credibility surface. Without it, the agency reads as "people who post about ads" not "people who make ads." Each case study should pull: client logo, before/after metric, specific deliverable, link to the campaign creative.

### 3. The story highlights are a frozen 2022 service deck — replicate AND date them on the website

The IG highlights ("Marketing Services / Strategy / Creative / Our Services") last updated October 11, 2022 — 3.5 years stale. **The website's services pages should mirror those four buckets but with 2026-fresh content:** specific deliverables, current pricing or "starting at" anchors, client examples per service. Then update the IG highlights to point at the website (story sticker → URL swipe).

### 4. The network grid is a goldmine the agency doesn't sell

The bio claims content production, brand strategy, marketing plans. **It does NOT mention "we own a 1.5M+ Arabic creator network."** The website should run a dedicated `/network` page that shows the family-handle inventory:
- @shalnack 672K
- @almobadir_mindset 478K
- @almobadir 362K
- @badrshaqer 317K
- @almobadir_flousak 202K
- @almobadir_business 125K
- @almobadir_media 58K

Sold as **"Influencer-Marketing-On-Tap"** (or Arabic: "تسويق المؤثرين تحت الطلب"). This is the single biggest commercially differentiated offer the agency has, and it is invisible from IG. The website can fix that in one page.

### 5. The agency profile is on a Creator account, not a Business account — the website should compensate with structured contact paths

Because the IG profile is Creator-tier, there's no Business inbox, no Contact button, no email/phone wiring. **The website becomes the de-facto contact infrastructure.** Place above-the-fold:
- A primary CTA "Book a strategy call" (Calendly inline)
- A secondary "Brief us on your project" form (5-7 fields max — name, brand, vertical, budget band, timeline)
- A WhatsApp Business deeplink (since DM-keyword ("تسويق") is the existing IG flow, mirror it on the site as a "Send 'تسويق' on WhatsApp" button)

### 6. The Maghrebi audience reality vs. pan-Arab pitch — the website needs a positioning fork

70% of top comments on viral posts are in Maghrebi Darija (Algerian, Moroccan dialect). The dragnet confirmed Morocco roots via the YouTube archive. **The website should run two service pitches:**
- **Maghreb-tier:** local brands, Darija-native creative, "we know what makes Casablanca/Algiers/Tunis click"
- **Khaleeji-tier (premium):** MSA / Khaleeji creative, regional campaign management, Riyadh-Dubai positioning (justifies higher CPMs)

This avoids the current misfit where bio reads "pan-Arab agency" but audience is Maghrebi and pricing implications are unclear.

### 7. The DM-keyword CTA is the only working funnel — make the website triple-redundant on that step

"Send 'تسويق' on الخاص" is the bio's only explicit conversion step. **Replicate that exact mechanic on the website in 3 forms:**
- WhatsApp deeplink with pre-filled message: "تسويق - أريد أن أبدأ مع Almobadir Media"
- Inline contact form with that as the prefilled subject
