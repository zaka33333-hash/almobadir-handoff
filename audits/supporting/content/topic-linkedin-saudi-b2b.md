## Executive summary

LinkedIn in Saudi Arabia hit **10.95 million users in Jan 2026** (28.5% of population) — large enough to function as Almobadir's primary B2B channel, small enough that voice still wins reach. ([NapoleonCat Jan 2026](https://stats.napoleoncat.com/linkedin-users-in-saudi_arabia/2026/01/), [Connectsafely 2026](https://connectsafely.ai/articles/linkedin-statistics-2026)). Saudi LinkedIn CPM averages **$10–22** vs $31–38 global median — Saudi is **30–55% cheaper than the global B2B baseline** for paid reach ([Stackmatix 2026](https://www.stackmatix.com/blog/linkedin-ads-cost), regional CPM data via [The Postiv](https://postiv.ai/blog/linkedin-advertising-costs)). Saudi B2B SAR-1,200 CPL is justifiable when deal sizes hit SAR 50K+, which an agency retainer easily clears.

The competitive picture: large network agencies (FP7 McCann 78.8K, BPG 41.4K, Memac Ogilvy 28K, M&C Saatchi MENA 9.3K) own the institutional layer; **independent Saudi creative shops sit at 8–17K followers** (Bassmat 8.2K, Rayz 16.7K, The Bold Group 6.8K). Almobadir at 0 is competing in a band where 2K is achievable in 90 days with founder-led content and ~$300/mo paid amplification. Saudi B2B buyers favor **carousels (6.6%-7% engagement, ~6× text-only), polls (4–8%), and native video** over outbound — and they want **Vision-2030-tied content** over generic thought leadership ([Edelman/LinkedIn 2025 B2B TL Report](https://www.edelman.com/expertise/Business-Marketing/2025-b2b-thought-leadership-report); [Expandway KSA B2B Strategy 2026](https://expandway.sa/b2b-content-strategy-ksa/); [Sprout Social](https://sproutsocial.com/insights/linkedin-carousels/)).

**Day-1 move: claim the `linkedin.com/company/almobadir-media` URL, fill 12 fields, post one Vision-2030-themed carousel.** Total time: 90 minutes.

### What separates the 40K+ tier from the 8K tier

1. **Cadence.** FP7 McCann posts daily and reposts client-side wins; Bassmat posts ~weekly. The 10× delta in cadence drives much of the follower delta.
2. **Employee advocacy.** FP7 has 599 employees liking/sharing on logged-in LinkedIn — a multiplier no Saudi indie can match in absolute terms but every indie can replicate proportionally.
3. **Award & PR pipeline.** Cannes Lions, Effies, MENA Effie, Campaign ME features feed the LinkedIn page; indies rarely tie award PR back to LinkedIn announcements.
4. **Recruiting funnel content.** Network agencies post jobs/culture content that compounds; indie agencies skip this and forfeit the steady follower drip from passive job-seekers.

### Indie-band benchmark (Almobadir's realistic peer set)

- **Bassmat (8.2K, 49 employees, est 2003)** — 23 years to 8K = ~360 followers/yr at the indie pace.
- **Rayz (16.7K, 49 employees, est 2012)** — 14 years to 16.7K = ~1,200 followers/yr; benefits from Vision-2030-era social-first positioning.
- **The Bold Group (6.8K)** — explicit "proud Saudi creative ecosystem" positioning resonates locally.

**Read for Almobadir.** A realistic 12-month target is **2,000–3,500 followers** with consistent cadence + founder-led amplification. Bassmat-tier (8K) is reachable in 24–30 months. Network-agency tier (40K+) requires either an exit/acquisition or a multi-year cadence Almobadir cannot currently sustain — and is the wrong target. Indie band is the prize.

### Saudi LinkedIn CPL framing

The SAR 1,200 (~$320) CPL number sounds high vs. the $110 global average. The framing that wins in Saudi B2B: **CPL × close rate × deal size = LTV ratio**. For Almobadir's likely retainer scale (SAR 15–60K/month, SAR 180–720K/yr), one closed deal pays back 150–600 CPLs. The threshold question is therefore close-rate, not CPL. ([Postiv 2026](https://postiv.ai/blog/linkedin-advertising-costs); [23HubLab GCC paid media](https://23hublab.com/paid-media-budget-allocation-across-gcc-markets/)).

### Recommended Almobadir paid posture

- **Months 1–2:** Pure organic. No ads. Post 12× / month, comment 30×, build 500–1,000 follower base.
- **Month 3:** Boost top 2 organic posts with $20–30 each (~SAR 75–110) per post — 4 boosts/month = SAR 400/mo.
- **Month 4+:** Single Lead Gen Form campaign at $50/day for 30 days = $1,500 (~SAR 5,600) with **expected 30–50 leads at $30–50 CPL** if the offer is sharp. Use Document Ads format (lowest CPL, [Stackmatix](https://www.stackmatix.com/blog/linkedin-ads-cost)).

### LinkedIn ROAS for B2B

LinkedIn reports B2B advertisers see **121% ROAS — $2.21 returned per $1 spent** ([LinkedIn Business 2025 B2B Benchmark](https://www.linkedin.com/business/marketing/blog/marketing-collective/2025-b2b-marketing-benchmar-the-video-influence-effect-starts-with-trust)). Self-reported by LinkedIn so discount accordingly, but the directional read is consistent with third-party sources.

**The flag.** The Almobadir public bio claims "MBA-Marketing". Badr's LinkedIn at `/in/badr-shaqer-2a1a28241` shows no Education entries, no degree, no institution.
