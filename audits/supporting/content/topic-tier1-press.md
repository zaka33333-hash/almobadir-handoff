## Executive summary (≈250 words)

Almobadir is one of the most owned-media-rich Arabic brands on the open web (≈3.3M cumulative followers across @almobadir 318K, @almobadir_mindset 341K, @almobadir_business 108K, @almobadir_media 60K, founder @badrshaqer 325K, plus four TikToks, YouTube, Threads). Yet the Wave-1 dragnet found ZERO tier-1 Arabic press coverage — not a single article, interview, panel, or podcast guesting on Asharq Business, Argaam, Al Eqtisadiah, Forbes ME, Wamda, Sayidaty, SPA, Athar, LEAP, Misk or Thmanyah ([dragnet-ar.md](./dragnet-ar.md)). This is a distribution moat in the wrong direction: a brand running a paid-acquisition agency on top of 3.3M consumer followers has zero third-party validation in its actual market.

The plan in this document fixes that asymmetry in 90 days. The earned-media universe Almobadir actually needs to dent breaks into three layers: (1) Tier-1 Arabic business press — Wamda, Asharq Business with Bloomberg, Forbes Middle East, Entrepreneur Al Arabiya, Argaam, Asharq Al-Awsat, Al Eqtisadiah, AMEinfo, MEED, Inc. Arabia. (2) Saudi-specific outlets — SPA, Sayidaty, Riyadh Daily, Al-Iqtisadiah TV. (3) Podcasts and stages — ABtalks (Anas Bukhash), Fnjan (Thmanyah), Sawalef Business, The Mo Show, plus the Saudi Media Forum (Feb 2026), 1 Billion Followers Summit (Jan 2026), LEAP (Aug 2026), Misk Global Forum (autumn 2026), Athar Festival (Oct 2026), FII (Oct 2026), Step Conference (Feb 2026), Dubai Lynx (Apr 2026).

The single highest-leverage move: **secure a Wamda profile of Badr Shaker as the case-study founder for the August 2025 Wamda × Inc. Arabia storytelling collaboration** ([Wamda × Inc. Arabia announcement](https://www.wamda.com/2025/08/wamda-inc-arabia-collaborate-strengthen-startup-storytelling-mena)). One placement = co-publication across both flagships and the regional credibility unlock that closes the earned-media-zero gap.

## Open questions for Badr

These are the unresolved variables the press cycle hinges on. Answer these before Phase 1 ships.

1. **Date of birth?** Forbes ME 30 Under 30 cut-off is "born 1995 or later" for the most recent cycle ([Forbes ME 30 Under 30 nominations](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/forbesmiddleeast_nominations-are-officially-open-for-forbes-activity-7350558451225317376-9irg)). If outside, target the broader "100 Most Inspiring Leaders" / "100 Most Powerful Arab Family Businesses" / forthcoming creator-specific lists.
2. **Are the agency self-stats audited?** "1.5M followers / 25M monthly views / $1M paid spend / 143+ clients / 5,000+ pieces" — are these third-party-verifiable, or self-reported? If self-reported, a HypeAuditor or Phyllo audit ($500–$2,000) before pitching unlocks Tier-1.
3. **Who else is on the team?** The Wave-1 dragnet identified only "A. بدر" on the agency about page and zero LinkedIn presence ([dragnet Row 11](./dragnet-ar.md)). For Forbes / Wamda profiles, the editorial standard requires named co-founders/leadership. Is there an "all-hands" team page Badr is willing to publish?
4. **Is the "شلناك" (Shalnack) creative-director role real, and if so, is the person open to being named?** Dragnet [Standout Find #5](./dragnet-ar.md) couldn't independently verify this hire. If real, it's another founder-narrative asset; if not, remove from the brand story.
5. **Has Badr ever appeared on a podcast / panel / TV interview that didn't index?** The dragnet found zero. If even one exists (Snapchat live, IG Live with another creator, Saudi YouTube channel guesting), it's a referenceable proof-point that drops the cold-pitch friction by an order of magnitude.
6. **Is نشرة المبادر going to ship for real in 2026, or be removed?** Before Phase 2 pitches go out, a decision is required (per the website-changes section above). Both paths work — but the ambiguous status does not.
7. **Is there already a Saudi PR retainer in place?** If yes, this plan should be handed to them as a brief, not executed independently. If no, the budget question is: does Almobadir spend $3,000 in-house OR retain a Saudi PR firm at $4–8K/month for the first 90 days?

If not raising, the press cycle is sequenced around content milestones (e.g., "5,000th piece of content shipped" or "newsletter relaunch"). The trigger determines pitch order.
9. **Forbes ME family-business angle — is there a parent company "Almobadir Holding" or similar?** The dragnet implied "almobadir.com" + "almobadir.media" are sister entities. If there's a parent, "100 Most Powerful Arab Family Businesses" becomes available even if Badr is age-ineligible for 30 Under 30.
10. **Vision-2030-mapped pitch — is Almobadir registered with Misk Foundation, GEA (General Entertainment Authority), or any government creator-program?** If yes, leverage immediately for SPA wire pickup. If no, the application path is via Misk Hub ([hub.misk.org.sa](https://hub.misk.org.sa/)) + GEA initiatives ([gea.gov.sa/initiatives](https://www.gea.gov.sa/initiatives/)).
